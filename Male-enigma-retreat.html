Male enigma retreat

| Posted on | 19 August, 2023

The Program founded by Pastor Moriss Gacheru some years ago was meant to revolutionize the spiritual, Mental and Emotional well being of Men. The Male enigma is usually held every saturday of a stipulated date of the month. On this day they were marking 2 years anniversary since start of the program. Being a member of the program for a while so I had to avail myself for the event.

fuchsia Gardens, limuru was the location of the event attended by over 700 men of all age. Yes “all age” , boys and men in their youth and old age from all walk of life were in attendance. Personally what caught my attention was the weather, been long since I have been to a cold region. Limuru Weather is on another level arrival we were welcome with aerobic exercise and breakfast to begin the activities of the day.

Breakfast Time

The Theme of the Day

Having all the day the event was meant to end at 5:00pm, the line up of the program was characterised by a series of activities, that include mentoring sessions , worship sessions, aerobic exercise sessions, series of games and most importantly men coming together before the presence of God.

“Healing, Strengthening and Mentoring the Men”

Mentoring session

It started by a worship and praying sessions then we sat down in the thick lawn of fuchsia garden, quite comforting I must say though some end up choosing to sit on the chair. Then we were engaged by mentoring sessions Lead by Pastor Mwangi of Jubilee Christian Church, Nakuru. Something that I was looking forward to, Topic was about Regrets one is faced with as they grow older. Honestly commenting it was quite informative. Personally I was caught by surprise about the praise and worship sessions. I never that will happen provided we were in sporty atmosphere. That shows how much they are dedicated to Christ.

Praise and worship sessions

To give you a snippet of the Topic, some of the Regrets include;

Not doing more for others.

not helping the people that you could help

Not building good relationships

Praying on your life vs praying for life

Not eating healthier

obviously, but then do you eat healthier?

Holding grudges

Is there anyone in your life, who you haven't forgiven. still Holding vengeance?

Not spending time with your Kids

I will leave that for you to meditate on it

Everyone surely left the event with a renewed mind,

Games and food

As much it was an event organised under a church setting, It was still packed with a lot of activities I mean the interaction, games and the engagement is something that someone will look upto, so the Games include Tug of war, filling bottle with water and Running with a sack. The teams were selected the first thing we entered the main entrance. Everyone of us was given a ribbon of different color tied in the wrist, I was with Team Red, which I must say we were quite standing out, like dah!! We were a vibe. Beside the point, the teams were competitive and engaging. Team Yellow end up the winners of Male enigma 2nd anniversary, they were awarded a Trophy.

Nyama choma and Ugali, offcourse!

Safari na leteipan we are so big in Food, I mean sampling different African Cuasinie and submit our review then sharing with You is Our goals, this event was indeed organised In term of logistics, kudos to the organising committee involve. personally I had blast. Enough with the confession let’s jump straight to the
Menu of the day;

  1. • Mahamri, Sweet potatoe and banana served with tea & coffee for Breakfast.
  2. • Nyama choma (roast meat), fried meat,veggies (spinach)served with Ugali for Lunch
  3. • Beef soup, African Sausage (mutura)goat head and served with Ugali for Dinner

The serving was done per team by the team leader.

Indeed we had a blast At the Male enigma 2nd anniversary Retreat. hope to engage in the event more. By the way Male enigma is held every saturday of the month at Jubilee Christian Church, Thika Road.
