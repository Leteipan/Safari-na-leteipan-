The Infamous hidden gem in kajiado

| Posted on | 19 August, 2023

Ever had of “munjiriri”, I guess No!!, no worries as Safari na leteipan we are here to ‘familiarise’ you, or what is the term? So munjiriri is a forest in Kajiado County,Kenya. Sholinke ward. This was not initially a forest but a valley but with time the vegetation outgrow the valley to become a forest, Nature did it again!, though the name ‘Munjiriri’ we are yet to know the meaning..

According to the community around, most of the information we got, is the place is a no Go Zone!, why?, Read further to find out.

History

Apparently from our knowledge is quite old and over the years the vegetation keep growing and covering up the place. Now to what caught our attention, the community around which are mostly Maasai say that the place is a No go Zone because it is believed there is a huge snake that feed on livestock and yet never seen, but then we went inside and not witness anything. Let’s just say it is superstition, No?

“There is a huge snake that feed on livestock ”

The Ecosystem

The place is so serene, there are various species of Birds, Animals especially we witnessed the a wild jackal, that somehow scared us luckily we chase them away from getting closer to us with stones, yeah with stones; apart from at the time we visited the place the river was dry so we got a chance to experience the rocks where the river flow. The river gain it’s flow the plains area. plus the trees and grass make the adventure much worth it especially getting inside was ultimate challenge.

Direction

To get here, you need to first check in with the authority, the area chief will do. Since the place is not yet declared a tourist destination. So it will be wise to also connect with a local to take you through. You can contact us for more information.

The places was worth the Tour, though don't go alone, it can get scary. But then if this kind of 'scray' adventure excites you, why not.
