Coolest things to do in Karura forest

| Posted on | 19 August, 2023

Karura is a forest located 8km from the CBD(Nairobi City),Hence makes it an urban forest. We had an opportunity to visit this magnificent forest with mostly planted trees and natural tree. The Scenery view is one to cherish.

Entrance fee

After 25 minutes drive from the city we are welcome in the Gate by Kenya wildlife service officers as we pay an entrance fee of $2(200 kshs) as a citizen.

Things to do

They are a lot of things to do including bicycle riding, running and Touring the waterfalls.

Bicyle Riding

Being a large forest with well maintain Trails one can cycle around with comfort plus they are well marked so you are sure to be on the right track. The charges is 500 kshs per bicycle. The bicycle are well serviced. Good mountain bike for sure.

Walking

Of course walking is an activity especially if you are doing it inside nature. Though something to note, people are not allowed inside the forest coverages only the allowed marked area. Which includes the waterfalls and lakes

Reading

Are you a reading enthusiast, well the forest will be a good place to elevate your reading experience. Karura management has been considerate in provision of chill spot with wooden relaxing chairs. Where you can read with all the serene and quite surrounding.

Horse Riding

Horse riding in a forest, feels alluring right. Well there is also horse riding in the forest with guide official, a horse ride goes for 500 kshs. You could do that Indiana jones thing.

Nature and wildlife

There is plenty of Nature to experience in karura forest, including a cave with a history to it (hiding spot for freedom fighters in the 60s). Also several lake, We checked one of them by the name Lilly lake which is a man-made lake form from a mining activity. There is also a waterfall, which I must say is far from the main entrance.

Lily Lake

I couldn’t mention the Nature and forget to mention the Late Wangari Maathai an environment activist. She pioneer most of the tree planted in karura forest.

Trail path

Food and Restaurant

To my favourite segment, so one may think is hard to find a restaurant inside a forest but not Karura forest. River cafe is the name of the restaurant, located a few meters from the parking area. I try their bugger and mango juice, not impressed though. I will give the restaurant a 5/10, reason being, they didn’t have much of African Menu. They were more westernised. Don’t get me wrong I love me some western meals.
